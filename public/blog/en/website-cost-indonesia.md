Understanding the real website cost before you start is one of the more useful things you can do. The range is genuinely wide — from a few hundred thousand rupiah per year for a self-built site to hundreds of millions for enterprise custom development — and most of that variation is explainable once you know what drives it.

This guide breaks down every cost component: domain, hosting, design, development, and ongoing maintenance. By the end, you should have a clear number in mind before talking to any developer or agency.

## The fixed costs every website pays

Before you get to design or development, there are two unavoidable costs: a domain name and hosting. These apply regardless of how you build your site.

### Domain name

A domain is your address on the web (yourbusiness.com). Pricing varies by extension:

| Domain extension | Annual cost |
|-----------------|-------------|
| .com | Rp 99,000 – Rp 200,000 |
| .id | Rp 200,000 – Rp 250,000 |
| .co.id | Rp 150,000 – Rp 300,000 |

Source: <a href="https://tonjoo.com/id/biaya-pembuatan-website/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Tonjoo Website Cost Guide 2025</a>

Domain prices are relatively stable year to year. The bigger variable is the registrar you choose, so compare a few before buying.

### Hosting

Hosting is the server where your website's files actually live. Your options:

| Hosting type | Annual cost | Best for |
|-------------|-------------|----------|
| Shared hosting | Rp 155,000 – Rp 420,000 | Personal sites, low traffic |
| Cloud hosting | Rp 900,000 – Rp 1.35 million | Business sites, moderate traffic |
| Managed WordPress | Rp 1.5M – Rp 5M+ | Sites needing performance and support |

Source: <a href="https://tonjoo.com/id/biaya-pembuatan-website/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Tonjoo Website Cost Guide 2025</a>

Shared hosting is cheap but limited. If your site is doing real work for your business, cloud hosting is the minimum worth considering.

## Website cost by build method

How you build your site is the biggest single driver of cost. There are three main routes.

### DIY website builders

Platforms like <a href="https://www.wix.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Wix</a> and <a href="https://wordpress.com/pricing/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">WordPress.com</a> let you build a site yourself using drag-and-drop tools. No developer needed.

**WordPress.com plan pricing (billed annually):**

| Plan | Monthly cost |
|------|-------------|
| Free | Rp 0 |
| Personal | ~$9/month |
| Premium | ~$18/month |
| Business | ~$40/month |
| Commerce | ~$70/month |

Source: <a href="https://wordpress.com/pricing/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">WordPress.com pricing page</a>

The trade-off: you get an affordable starting point, but you spend time learning the platform, working within template constraints, and handling your own troubleshooting. For validating an idea or putting up a basic presence, this works well. For a site that's meant to drive serious business growth, you'll hit limits.

### WordPress with a freelancer or agency

Most small-business websites are built on <a href="https://wordpress.org" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">WordPress.org</a> (self-hosted) by a freelancer or small agency. This gives you more flexibility than a builder while costing less than fully custom development.

Typical price ranges for professional WordPress builds:

| Business size | Website cost |
|--------------|-------------|
| Small business (5–10 pages) | Rp 2.5M – Rp 8M |
| Medium business with features | Rp 7.5M – Rp 75M |
| Large company / corporate | Rp 80M – Rp 250M |

Source: <a href="https://badoystudio.com/berapa-biaya-membuat-website/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Badoy Studio website cost breakdown</a>

The wide range within each tier reflects design complexity, number of pages, and what features are needed. A basic five-page company profile site is very different from a site with membership, booking, or e-commerce functionality.

### Custom development

Fully custom websites — built without a CMS template — are for businesses with specific technical requirements, non-standard workflows, or where the website is the product itself (web apps, platforms, portals).

**E-commerce sites specifically:**

| Complexity | Website cost |
|-----------|-------------|
| Basic e-commerce | Rp 10M – Rp 35M |
| Full-featured store | Rp 35M – Rp 100M |
| Enterprise / marketplace | Rp 100M+ |

Source: <a href="https://badoystudio.com/berapa-biaya-membuat-website/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Badoy Studio website cost breakdown</a>

Developer hourly rates for custom work run around <a href="https://www.hostinger.com/id/tutorial/estimasi-biaya-pembuatan-website" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Rp 1.1 million to Rp 2.5 million per hour</a>. Scoping your project carefully before starting is the main way to control these costs.

## Hidden costs that catch people off guard

The domain and hosting numbers are the easy part. These are the costs that don't show up in a basic quote.

### SSL certificates

An SSL certificate encrypts data between your site and visitors. Basic certificates come free with most hosting plans. Paid certificates run Rp 100,000 to Rp 20 million per year, with the high end covering enterprise and EV certificates for financial or legal businesses. For most sites, a free SSL from your host is fine.

### Premium themes and plugins

If you're using WordPress, expect ongoing plugin costs. A single premium plugin can cost Rp 100,000 to Rp 1 million or more per year. Popular plugin bundles can run upward of Rp 10 million annually. Read the fine print before buying, and audit what you actually use each year.

### Copywriting and content

A professionally written page costs Rp 50,000 to Rp 500,000 per article, depending on length and expertise. Many businesses underestimate this until they're staring at empty page templates. If you're writing it yourself, budget the time.

### Maintenance

Regular updates, security patches, and backups are not optional. Self-managed maintenance runs Rp 0 to Rp 1.35 million per year if you do it yourself. Professional maintenance services start around Rp 70,000 per month and can reach Rp 5 million per month for high-availability sites. Source: <a href="https://www.hostinger.com/id/tutorial/estimasi-biaya-pembuatan-website" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Hostinger website cost guide</a>.

## What actually drives the price up

Two websites with the same page count can have very different price tags. Here's why.

### Design complexity

A unique brand identity with custom illustrations, animations, and a distinct visual language takes significantly more time than dropping content into a template. The more design rounds, the higher the cost.

### Functionality requirements

Payment integrations, booking systems, membership portals, inventory management, and API connections all add development time. Each feature is its own scoping, building, and testing cycle.

### Platform choice

<a href="https://wordpress.org" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">WordPress</a> builds typically cost less than fully custom-coded solutions. Regulated industries — healthcare, finance, legal — often need enhanced security compliance, which adds to development scope regardless of platform.

### Who builds it

A solo freelancer and a full-service agency can produce similar outputs with very different price tags and timelines. Freelancers generally cost less but may have longer turnaround or limited availability. Agencies bring more resources but come with overhead baked into the rate. Neither is inherently better; it depends on your project complexity.

## Frequently asked questions

**Q: What is a realistic website cost for a small business?**
A: A professional small-business website typically runs between Rp 7.5 million and Rp 25 million for design and development, plus ongoing hosting and domain fees. If you use a website builder and set it up yourself, you can get started for under Rp 500,000 per year, though you'll invest more time.

**Q: Why does website cost vary so much between providers?**
A: Website cost depends on who builds it (freelancer vs. agency), what platform they use, how much custom design is involved, and how many features you need. A five-page brochure site is a very different project from an e-commerce store with inventory management and payment integration.

**Q: What ongoing website costs should I budget for each year?**
A: At minimum, budget for domain renewal (around Rp 99,000–Rp 200,000/year) and hosting (Rp 155,000–Rp 1.3 million/year depending on type). If you want security certificates, premium plugins, SEO tools, or professional maintenance, annual costs can reach several million rupiah.

**Q: Is it worth paying more for a custom website over a template?**
A: It depends on your business stage. Templates work well when you're validating an idea or have a straightforward offer. Custom design makes sense when your brand differentiation matters, you need specific functionality, or the site is a primary sales channel for your business.

**Q: How do I avoid overpaying for a website?**
A: Get clear on what your site needs to do before talking to any developer. A written brief prevents scope creep and makes quotes comparable. Avoid paying for complexity you don't need — a clean five-page site often outperforms an over-engineered one. Read our <a href="/insights/how-to-write-a-website-brief-for-a-developer">guide to writing a website brief</a> for a practical starting point.

**Q: What is the cheapest way to get a website cost estimate?**
A: The fastest way is to use a website builder like Wix or WordPress.com and check their published pricing directly. For custom development, sharing a written brief with two or three developers will get you quotes you can actually compare.
